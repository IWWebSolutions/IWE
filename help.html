Frequently Asked Questions

1. General Questions

Q1. What is India World Exports?
India World Exports is a B2B platform that connects Indian exporters, manufacturers, and suppliers with global buyers.

2. Registration and Account Management

Q2. How do I register as a seller/exporter?
Visit our website and click on the "Register" button. Fill in the required details and submit the registration form.

3. Product Listings

Q3. How do I list my products?
After registration, you can add products through your seller dashboard with detailed descriptions and images.

4. Payments and Shipping

Q4. What payment methods are accepted?
We support bank transfers, letters of credit, and other secure payment methods.
